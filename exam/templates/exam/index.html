Guidelines for Online Exam.

  • Before beginning the exam:
    1. Make sure you have a good internet connection.
    2. Log out of Blacboard and then back in to Blackboard before you begin.This will help with connectivity issues.
    3. If you are taking the exam late in the day, it is recommended that you reboot your computer before beginning to free up memory resources from other programs on your computer.
    4. Shut down all Instant messaging tools (Skype,AIM,MSN Messenger) and Email programs as they can conflict with the Test.
    5. Enter the Test by clinking the link below.
    6. Do not use any other internet browser.
    7. Maximise your browser window before at starting the test.
    8. Minimising the browser window during the exam can prevent the submission of your exam.
    9. When you begin the exam click the link only ONCE to launch the test . Double Clicking can lock the test.
  • During the Exam:
    1. Do not resize(minimise) the browser during test.
    2. Never click the "Back" button on the browser.This will take you out of the test and prevent test from tracking your selected answers.
    3. Avoid using scroll button on your mouse if present.
    4. Click the "submit" button to submit your exam. Do not press "Enter" on the keyboard to submit the exam.
